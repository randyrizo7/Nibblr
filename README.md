
# Nibblr

Nibblr is a social food-discovery app that helps people decide where to eat together.  
Using swipe-based interactions, Nibblr lets users swipe on restaurants, match with friends, and surface shared favorites without endless back-and-forth.

Think: swipe-based discovery combined with taste-driven social signals, built specifically for decision-making.

---

## Problem Statement

Choosing a place to eat is surprisingly difficult, especially with groups.

- Group chats stall with indecision
- Recommendation platforms optimize for ratings, not people
- Too many choices lead to decision fatigue

Nibblr focuses on decisions rather than browsing by making preferences explicit and highlighting overlap between people.

---

## Core Features

### Restaurant Swiping
- Swipe right to like, left to pass
- Low-effort preference input
- Supports solo and group discovery modes

### Friend and Group Matching
- Match with friends based on shared likes
- Instantly see overlapping restaurant preferences
- Designed for couples, roommates, and groups

### Social Discovery
- View friendsâ€™ top restaurants by cuisine or category
- See restaurants favorited by the most groups
- Discover trending spots within your social network
- Emphasizes taste overlap over popularity or influence

### Restaurant Discovery
- Location-aware restaurant suggestions
- Supports filters such as distance, cuisine, and price
- Balances exploration with familiar favorites

### Taste Profiles (Planned)
- Persistent record of food preferences
- Personal and social taste summaries
- Designed to inform recommendations without follower counts

### Decision Randomizer
- Randomly selects from mutual likes
- Useful for date nights or group outings

## Design Philosophy

- Decisions over discovery  
  Help users choose efficiently instead of browsing endlessly.

- Social without metrics  
  No follower counts, likes, or engagement scores.

- Taste-driven signals  
  Restaurants surface based on shared preferences and group behavior, not global ratings.

- Lightweight by default  
  Reduce friction and cognitive load.
